About the Author

Δ

Sometimes I wonder if happiness has ever chosen me. No matter how deeply I love, I always end up in partings with the ones I thought would stay. The heartbreaks pile up, silently—too heavy to carry, too fragile to tell.

Writing became the only place where I could leave these unsaid words, where sorrow could exist without shame. Every page I create is a reflection of the storms inside me, a quiet confession dressed in letters.

If you are here, reading this, maybe you too have carried a weight that no one else could see. Maybe my words will echo a little of your silence, and remind you that even broken hearts still deserve to be heard.

— DX Atelier
